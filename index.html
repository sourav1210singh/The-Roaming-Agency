Frequently Asked Questions

A roaming band, equally known as a strolling band, is an unplugged and therefore mobile group. Musicians perform while mingling with guests throughout the venue without microphones, offering interactive performances.

The standard offering consists of 3 sets of 30 minutes each, with the possibility of spreading them out between the key stages of the event. Larger or premium events can book extended options: 4 sets of 30 minutes or 3 sets of 40 minutes.

Our repertoire spans a wide variety of songs, in different languages and in all styles. We adapt performances to each unique audience and recommend trusting us musically once festivities begin, though specific titles can be requested.

We live in Nice, France, on the beautiful French Riviera.

Anywhere and everywhere our music is needed!

A strolling band has no strong amplification. The concept involves bringing the music directly to the guests. The acoustic setup performs loud enough to get over 150 people dancing without DJ-level volume.

Being acoustic, there is no need for access to a power source. A hot meal onsite and digital content from photographers/videographers are appreciated.
